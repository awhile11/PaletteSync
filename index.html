About This Project

Designed on the
foundations of color theory.

This website was created with a simple goal: to make finding the right color combinations fast, intuitive, and accessible to everyone.

Instead of relying on artificial intelligence, this tool is built on the foundations of real color theory. Every palette is generated using proven principles such as complementary, analogous, and triadic relationships — combined with carefully balanced adjustments to saturation and lightness to ensure visually pleasing results.

The idea is simple: you choose a color, and the system intelligently builds a palette that works. Whether you're designing a website, creating a brand, or just experimenting with colors, this tool helps you move from guesswork to confident design decisions.

This project reflects a deeper understanding of how colors interact, not just how they look. By focusing on structure, balance, and usability, it aims to provide palettes that are not only correct — but actually feel right.

Built for designers, developers, and creators who want clean, reliable color combinations — without overcomplicating the process.

01
Complementary
Colors opposite on the hue wheel (180°) create maximum contrast and visual energy.
02
Analogous
Adjacent hues (±30°) produce harmonious, nature-like palettes that feel cohesive.
03
Triadic
Three hues equally spaced (+120°, +240°) offer rich variety while keeping balance.
04
Monochromatic
Multiple lightness levels of a single hue create depth and sophisticated tonal layers.
